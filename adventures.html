skiing at the beautiful Beaver Mountain

At the beginning of 2024, I faced my fears and went skiing for the first time. As an Arizona girl, I'm used to sunshine and pools, not the cold snow. I worked hard to learn the skills. After falling countless times, I pushed through and got better!

The beautiful City of St. Louis, Missouri

From 2022-2023, I spent time in St. Louis, MO as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I spent my days talking to people and helping them in any way I could. I learned a lot of life lessons that have prepared me for my life. This was one of my hardest but greatest adventures.

Boating, Swimming, Fishing in Lake Powell, Utah

I spent my summer in 2020 on a houseboat with my family in the gorgeous Lake Powell. This piece of heaven provides countless opportunities for adventure and fun. I spent my time wakeboarding, surfing, fishing, and spending time with the people most important to me. I can't wait for my next trip back!
